About This App

The Statistical Analysis Suite is a powerful tool designed to help researchers, analysts, and students explore their data and understand the impact of programs, policies, or business changes. Simply upload your dataset and let the app guide you through a comprehensive analysis.

Features

  • Upload any CSV or Excel dataset for analysis
  • Descriptive statistics with summary tables and visualizations
  • Inferential statistics including t-tests, ANOVA, and regression
  • Causal impact analysis using difference-in-differences
  • Synthetic control method implementation
  • Interactive charts and exportable data tables
  • LLM-powered chatbot for guided interpretation
