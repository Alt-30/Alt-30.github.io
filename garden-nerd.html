About Garden Nerd

Garden Nerd is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help gardeners in the Arizona desert climate. Whether you're a beginner or experienced gardener, get instant answers to your plant care questions.

What You Can Ask

  • Watering schedules for specific plants
  • Best plants for Arizona climate zones
  • Pest identification and management
  • Seasonal planting calendars
  • Soil preparation and amendments
  • Heat stress prevention tips

Pro Tips

Be specific about your location within Arizona for the most accurate advice. Mention your USDA hardiness zone if you know it.

Include details about your current setup (container vs. in-ground, sun exposure, soil type) for personalized recommendations.
