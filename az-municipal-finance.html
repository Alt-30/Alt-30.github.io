About This Dashboard

The Arizona Municipal Finance Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of fiscal data for cities and towns across the state. Users can compare municipalities on various financial metrics and understand how local governments allocate resources.

Features

  • Compare multiple municipalities side-by-side
  • Analyze revenue sources and expenditure categories
  • Track fiscal trends over time
  • Download data for further analysis
  • Interactive filtering and visualization
  • Per capita and aggregate metrics

Data Sources

Arizona Auditor General
Municipal Annual Reports
U.S. Census Bureau
