About This App

The Census Data Explorer provides easy access to U.S. Census Bureau data products. Select variables, geographic levels, and time periods to explore demographic, economic, housing, and social data across the nation.

Features

  • Pull data from American Community Survey (ACS) 1-year and 5-year estimates
  • Explore multiple geographic levels: nation, state, county, city/place
  • Compare data across multiple years
  • Interactive choropleth maps for geographic visualization
  • Dynamic charts and graphs for trend analysis
  • Exportable data tables for further analysis
  • Hundreds of demographic and socioeconomic variables
City to National Level: Drill down or zoom out across geographic boundaries
